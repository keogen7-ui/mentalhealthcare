About Us

Who we are

Mental Health Care Foundation is a social organization working to improve the emotional well-being of people — especially youth, students, and vulnerable communities.

We run educational talks, workshops, psychological counseling, and awareness campaigns. Everyone deserves emotional support and access to psychological care.

🎯 Mission

Promote mental health by providing support, education, and resources — reducing the stigma of talking about emotions.

🌎 Vision

A society where mental health is a priority and no one faces their emotional struggles alone.

The Problem

Mental health matters.

Many people suffer in silence due to fear, shame, or lack of resources. Young people are among the most affected.

01

Anxiety & Depression

Millions suffer without receiving care. Fear of stigma keeps them isolated and silent.

02

Pressure on Youth

Academic stress, family issues, social media and bullying profoundly affect young students.

03

Access Barriers

Shame, lack of information, and limited resources prevent many from seeking help.

04

Life Impact

Untreated mental health issues affect relationships, academics, quality of life, and physical health.
